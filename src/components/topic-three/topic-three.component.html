*ngClass & *ngStyle

Directive: [ngClass] & [ngStyle] Native: [class.name] & [style.prop]
Requires importing CommonModule, NgClass, or NgStyle into your component. Built directly into the core Angular template engine. No imports needed.
Evaluates objects, arrays, or strings. Example:
[ngClass]="{ 'bg-success': isActive }"		 Binds directly to a specific CSS class or style property. Example:
[class.bg-success]="isActive"
Best Use Case: Excellent when you need to apply multiple dynamic classes or styles at the exact same time based on complex logic. Best Use Case: Highly optimized and preferred for toggling single, specific classes or individual style properties.
