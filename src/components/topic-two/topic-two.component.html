*ngFor & @for

*ngFor Directive (Legacy) @for Block (Modern)
Requires importing CommonModule or NgFor into your component. Built directly into the template engine. No imports needed.
Performance tracking requires creating a custom trackBy function in your TypeScript file. Highly optimized by default. Includes a mandatory, simple track expression directly in the HTML.
Handling empty lists requires wrapping or accompanying the list with a separate *ngIf to check the array length. Highly readable and intuitive syntax with a native @empty block for zero-item states.
